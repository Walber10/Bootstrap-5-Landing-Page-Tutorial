Who we are


The club was founded in 2017 by Erick Santiago, who is the current club president.
in 2018 we had 25 players playing 3 competitions and we won 2 of them and next year 2019 we were already more them 60 players playing around 6 competitions. Our teams play in the Eastern Suburbs Football Association with a partnersthip with Maroura United: Men's Premier League, firsts and reserves, the top level of amateur football in Sydney Women's amateur football in Sydney If you are interested in joining the club, contact us via the links on the right. Through passionate management and community/sponsor support, players and members can enjoy football all year round and there is a place for anyone regardless of talent, age or gender. We proudly support each other through multiple social and fundraising events. Thanks to all our sponsors, committee members and supporters who keep us running!
